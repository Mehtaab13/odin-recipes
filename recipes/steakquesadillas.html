Steak Quesadillas

Description

We will be making Steak Quesadillas that are cheesy, meaty, and all around irresstiable. Enought mouth-watering, let's get right into it!

Ingredients

  • 4oz Costco Sirlion Steak Strips
  • 1 medium size Flour Tortilla
  • 3/4 cups of Mexican Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of Butter
  • 2 tablespoons of Chipotle Mayo

Steps

  1. Get a non stick pan and heat it up on low-medium heat for about two minutes
  2. Put a tablespoon of butter on to the pan
  3. Put the steak strips onto the pan and heat each side for about 1 minute
  4. Heat the tortilla on one side for about two minutes in the steak juices on the pan
  5. Flip the tortilla and add the cheese on the whole side and the steak on one half
  6. Apply the chipotle mayo on the steak and fold the tortilla
  7. Heat each side on the pan for about 30 seconds
